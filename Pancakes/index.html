Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • milk
  • Flour
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • oil

Steps

  1. Crack 2 eggs in a medium sized bowl, add 200ml of milk. After keep adding flour and stir till the mixture is semi thick
  2. Turn hob on to heat the pan. Add a small bit of oil before each time you put the mixture on. Using a spatula check if the bottom part of the pancake is golden. If yes then flip to fry the other side. Repeat this for the rest of the pancake dough.
  3. Once done, enjoy your pancakes with any jam of your desire, syrup or chocolate spread.

Other Info

Utensils

  • Bowl, Pan, Spatula

Time to cook

  • 30 minutes
