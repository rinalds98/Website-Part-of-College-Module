Frittata

Ingredients

  • 2 Bacon strips (diced)
  • Oil
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
  • 4 mushrooms (thinly sliced)
  • 1 tomato (diced)
  • 4 eggs
  • Pinch of salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, salt and pepper in a bowl.
  3. In a oven-proof pan, cook bacon in oven till crispy. Add mushrooms and stir occasionally, until tender and browned. Add pepper and salt.
  4. Add tomato and after stir in the egg mixture and bacon. Cook undisturbed for 2 minutes until edges are set.
  5. Place in overn and bake until top is set and golden brown.
  6. Server immediately

Other Info

Utensils

  • Bowl, Pan, Spatula

Time to cook

  • 30 minutes
