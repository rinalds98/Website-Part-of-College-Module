Vegetable soup

Ingredients

  • 1 table spoon of butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 litres of vegetable stock
  • Pinch of salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Place the butter in a large pot and place over a medium high heat. When the butter is melted, add the onion and potato. Fry for 2 minutes, then cover and steam for 8 minutes.
  2. Add the stock and carrots, lower the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender when you insert a knife.
  3. Season with a little salt and pepper to taste and then either serve as a chunky vegetable soup or use a hand blender to form a silky smooth soup.

Other Info

Utensils

  • Saucepan, Pot

Time to cook

  • 40 minutes
